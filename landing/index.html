About Us

Meet your experts in building.

Our very experienced team guarantee you security and a high quality of service and making us a strong partner for your project. Each year we manage construction projects with a total value of over then €1 billion.
A stable system of values and an awareness of the importance of a long-term perspective are also necessary in order for these principles to guide our daily work. DELTA operates with a corresponding vision and that is also reflected in our values.
